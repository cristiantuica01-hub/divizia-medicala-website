About us

We support private clinics
in Romania

Divizia Medicală supports private clinics in Romania in building stable, functional teams. We act as an extension of your team, providing continuous support in an industry where recruitment is often done reactively, without structure and under operational pressure.

From front desk and patient care to medical assistants and physicians, we act as an external recruitment partner — with the goal of relieving teams from the burden of recruitment and allowing them to focus on what truly matters: medical care.

HOW WE WORK

We act as an extension of your team.

We start by understanding the clinic's structure, how the team is organized and the challenges encountered in recruitment. We analyze the clinic's market positioning, candidate expectations and the reasons why certain roles are harder to fill or retain long-term.

Starting from here, we define the recruitment approach and identify candidates who fit both the role requirements and the organization's working style.

We manage the relationship with candidates throughout the entire process, evaluating experience, motivation and compatibility with the team, so that only relevant profiles aligned with the clinic's budget and expectations reach the interview.

This way, the management team can focus their time on coordinating operations, patient experience and clinic development.

We help you save up to 10 hours for each active role.

1
